About Me

I'm a Digital Media professional with a background in broadcast, live production, post-production, and social media, supported by formal training in Broadcast and Cinematic Arts and hands-on experience across audio, video, and live event environments. I've worked on everything from studio-based educational content to large-scale live events, and I enjoy being involved throughout the full production process, from setup and capture to post-production and delivery. I bring a detail-oriented approach to my work, with a strong focus on technical accuracy, organized workflows, and creating media that's both polished and purposeful.

Beyond technical skills, I'm known as a competent, reliable collaborator who helps keep productions running smoothly under pressure. I lead through clear communication, adaptability, and a strong sense of responsibility to both the team and the project. Whether working with editors, engineers, stage crews, or on-air talent, I prioritize efficiency, safety, and collaboration, and I'm driven by a genuine passion for multimedia production and continuous growth in the field.
